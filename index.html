Donato Spinelli is a visual artist, developer and designer who specializes in the intersection of art and technology. He works as a freelancer by collaborating with artists, researchers and institutions such as Artport, We are here Venice, TBA21 and the Institute of Network Cultures. He graduated from the IUAV University of Venice with a Master's Degree in Visual Arts in 2020. Currently busy with the collective Slutty Urbanism, where he contributes as a designer and creative developer.
